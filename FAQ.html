Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers! 🤔

1. What is EcoPick?

EcoPick is an online store dedicated to eco-friendly products for daily use. We promote sustainable living through conscious consumption.

2. How do I place an order?

You can browse our product categories, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can also use the “Buy Now” option on any product.

3. What are your payment options?

We accept all major debit/credit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallet payments.

4. Can I track my order?

Yes, use our Track Order page and enter your order ID to view the delivery status.

5. What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unused and undamaged products. Please refer to our Refund Policy for full details.

6. How do I contact support?

Visit our Contact Us page to get in touch with our support team via form or email.
